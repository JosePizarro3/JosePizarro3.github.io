About me

I am a doctor in Physics by the Universidad Autónoma de Madrid, Spain since 2019. I am an expert in Materials Science, with a focus on Computational Methods applied to real materials. Since 2019, I worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Bremen, Hamburg, and Berlin.

I worked as a Software Developer and Data Manager. I also like to share my experience and mentor students. I also have collaborations from different countries around the world, and I am used to work in an international environment.
